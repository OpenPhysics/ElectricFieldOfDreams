# Credits — ElectricFieldOfDreams

SceneryStack port of the PhET 'Electric Field of Dreams' simulation. Place charged particles and observe Coulomb interactions and an adjustable uniform external field.

## Upstream inspiration

This is an independent [SceneryStack](https://scenerystack.org/) reimplementation inspired by a
[PhET Interactive Simulations](https://phet.colorado.edu/) title from the University of Colorado Boulder.
It is not an official PhET product. PhET® is a registered trademark of the University of Colorado.
